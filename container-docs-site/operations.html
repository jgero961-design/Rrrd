Legal & Operational Plan

LLC/SPV ownership, investor agreements, permits, reserve funds, and monthly reporting ensure robust governance and transparency.

Legal Structure

  • Form LLC/SPV owning land and improvements
  • Investor agreements cover distributions, transfers, and exit
  • Clear governance and operator mandate

Compliance

  • Obtain building permits and short-term rental approvals
  • Adhere to local zoning and fire safety regulations
  • Insurance coverage for construction and operation

Operations & Reporting

  • Reserve funds set aside for maintenance and unexpected expenses
  • Monthly investor reporting on occupancy, ADR, and P&L
  • Professional property management and guest services
