Unit Structure & Features

Container-based duplexes with garage below, living above, and rooftop veranda; each unit includes a private fenced yard.

Second Floor

Living area with kitchenette, bathroom, and bedroom; stairs to rooftop veranda.

Rooftop Veranda

Open-air leisure space with safety rails; designed for guest relaxation and panoramic views.

Private Yard

Each unit includes a fenced yard with space for a firepit and grill, plus pedestrian and car access gates.
