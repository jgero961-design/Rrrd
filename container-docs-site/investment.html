Ownership & Investment Model

Fractional ownership via SPV. Distributions are paid monthly in proportion to each investor’s share of equity.

Key Terms

  • Minimum buy-in: 1% (~₾5,569)
  • Operator management fee: 10% of gross
  • Operator equity: 10–20% retained
  • Monthly distributions proportional to ownership

Expected Returns

Estimated annual ROI after fees of approximately 48.7% based on current assumptions.

Cap Table & Transferability

Ownership is recorded within an SPV/LLC structure. Transfers, buybacks, and exit procedures are governed by investor agreements to ensure orderly changes of control.
